General

Siddharta is a Slovenian rock band formed in 1995, which, after twenty years of musical creativity, holds a prominent position as one of the most popular music groups in Slovenia. Named after the famous novel by German author Hermann Hesse, Siddharta has established itself as a significant presence in the Slovenian music scene, blending alternative rock, progressive rock, and alternative metal styles. The band's lineup has evolved over the years, with Tomi Meglič as the frontman, joined by Primož Benko, Jani Hace, Tomaž Okroglič-Rous, and Boštjan Meglič among others.

Music

Siddharta's journey is marked by successful albums showcasing evolving sound and lyrical depth. "Id" (1999) and "Nord" (2001) brought commercial success, featuring hit singles like "Pot v X" and "B Mashina." Subsequent releases like "Rh-" (2003) and "Petrolea" (2006) continued to push boundaries, exploring themes of identity and society through dynamic soundscapes and thought-provoking lyrics.

Awards

Siddharta has earned numerous awards and nominations, including the Viktor Award for music achievements and MTV Europe Music Awards nominations. Albums like "Rh-" and "Saga" received platinum and gold certifications respectively. Their innovative music videos have also received acclaim, solidifying their status as cultural icons in Slovenian music history.
